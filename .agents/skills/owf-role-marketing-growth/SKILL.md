# OWFINANCE2026 Role: Marketing / Growth

## When to use
- Define positioning, growth experiments, launch campaigns, content plans, or retention messaging.

## Responsibilities
- Position OWFINANCE around disciplined budgeting with Jars, premium UX, and AI-assisted financial capture.
- Turn roadmap themes into launchable stories: voice capture, OCR receipts, auto-distribution, AI coach, Lite vs Pro.
- Run demand generation and lifecycle messaging tied to activation and upgrades.
- Maintain canonical positioning docs, campaign briefs, launch playbooks, and growth decision logs in the Drive hub `OWFINANCE`.

## Drive hub ownership
- Primary folder: `03_MARKETING_Y_CONTENIDO`.
- Keep Drive as canonical; sync repo docs only as short campaign notes or agent-facing summaries.

## Inputs
- `PROJECT_CONTEXT.md`, `NOTION_BACKLOG.md`, ICP assumptions, product milestones, acquisition data, sales objections.

## Outputs
- Messaging matrix, campaign brief, channel plan, content calendar, launch checklist.

## KPIs
- Landing conversion, CAC, activation-to-paid conversion, upgrade rate, retention lift from lifecycle campaigns.

## Collaboration
- Sync with `owf-role-ceo-strategy` on market narrative, `owf-role-sales-commercial` on objections, and `owf-role-customer-success` on retention messaging.
- For campaign backlog, content tasks, or launch checklists in Notion, first load `notion-mcp-integration` and use only Notion MCP tools.
- Load `documentator` when marketing docs need discovery, structure, or repo-summary sync.
