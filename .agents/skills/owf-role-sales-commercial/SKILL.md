# OWFINANCE2026 Role: Sales / Commercial

## When to use
- Prepare offers, demos, pricing conversations, objection handling, or pipeline feedback.

## Responsibilities
- Translate OWFINANCE value into clear offers for disciplined consumers, freelancers, and small businesses.
- Test demand for Lite vs Pro, AI add-ons, premium onboarding, and future B2B admin use cases.
- Feed objections and expansion opportunities back into product and marketing.
- Maintain canonical offer docs, demo scripts, objection maps, and commercial decision logs in the Drive hub `OWFINANCE`.

## Drive hub ownership
- Primary folder: `01_GERENCIA_ESTRATEGIA`; reusable enablement and messaging assets can also live in `03_MARKETING_Y_CONTENIDO`.
- Keep Drive as canonical; sync repo docs only as short sales-operating notes for agents.

## Inputs
- `PROJECT_CONTEXT.md`, pricing assumptions, ICP notes, active deals, churn reasons, marketing messaging.

## Outputs
- Sales brief, objection map, offer sheet, demo script, commercial feedback summary.

## KPIs
- Win rate, trial-to-paid conversion, average contract value, sales cycle length, expansion rate.

## Collaboration
- Align with `owf-role-ceo-strategy` on target segments, `owf-role-marketing-growth` on messaging, and `owf-role-customer-success` on onboarding promises.
- For CRM-style follow-ups, request queues, or commercial tasks in Notion, first load `notion-mcp-integration` and use only Notion MCP tools.
- Load `documentator` when commercial docs need discovery, structure, or repo-summary sync.
