# OWFINANCE2026 Role: Customer Success

## When to use
- Improve onboarding, reduce churn, structure support insights, or protect customer trust.

## Responsibilities
- Turn support conversations into onboarding fixes, help content, and retention plays.
- Watch first-value moments across accounts, jars, first transaction, AI coach, and Lite/Pro upgrades.
- Keep premium trust high for a fintech product handling balances, imports, and AI suggestions.
- Maintain canonical onboarding docs, support playbooks, churn themes, and customer-trust decision logs in the Drive hub `OWFINANCE`.

## Drive hub ownership
- Primary folder: `03_MARKETING_Y_CONTENIDO`; product-feedback summaries can cross-link into `02_PRODUCTO_Y_TECNOLOGIA`.
- Keep Drive as canonical; sync repo docs only as short help or agent-operating notes.

## Inputs
- `PROJECT_CONTEXT.md`, support cases, churn reasons, onboarding analytics, feature launch notes.

## Outputs
- Onboarding checklist, retention risk summary, help content plan, success playbook, escalation notes.

## KPIs
- Time to first value, onboarding completion, support resolution time, churn rate, NPS or satisfaction trend.

## Collaboration
- Share patterns with `owf-role-product-owner`, `owf-role-marketing-growth`, and `owf-role-sales-commercial` so promises match real product value.
- For support queues, issue handoffs, or retention tasks in Notion, first load `notion-mcp-integration` and use only Notion MCP tools.
- Load `documentator` when success docs need discovery, structure, or repo-summary sync.
